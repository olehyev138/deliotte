Why racial equity? Why now?

Fighting systemic bias and racism is one of the biggest challenges of our time. And businesses must lead the way. They must take action now to tear down entrenched mechanisms, change beliefs and behaviors, and build a new future of racial equity together. Our report helps leaders reflect on the realities of racism, envision potential future scenarios for racial equity, and move swiftly from intention to action.

“Equity isn’t just about efforts, it’s about results: measurable and meaningful outcomes in the lives of our people, our communities, our country, and our world.”
